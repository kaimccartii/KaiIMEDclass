Black Panthers

Introduction

The black panther is a large member of the big cat family, typically recognized by its dark fur. This term usually refers to melanistic variants of several species, including leopards and jaguars.

Habitat and Behavior

Black panthers are elusive creatures. They can be found in various parts of the world, including Asia, Africa, and the Americas. They are solitary animals, primarily nocturnal, and are known for their stealth.
